About Me

Electrical Engineer

Hi, my name is Colton and I am currently a senior at ASU studying electrical engineering with an emphasis in signal processing and controls. I am interested in embedded systems and aerospace applications, and I have been very involved in the community at ASU working on collaborative, student-led projects and research. I am mainly involved with Sun Devil Rocketry, previously having held the positions of President and Liquid Propulsion lead. I also founded the Avionics Team, which I started in order to apply my interests in electronics and software to ongoing projects within the organization.

My technical work has primarily revolved around the development of the embedded controls that are needed to interface with mechanical systems and process sensor data. I am particularly interested in microcontrollers and SoCs, as I focus my projects on the application of these chips to aerospace systems and propulsion devices. Look at the projects posted below for more information.

Skillset

PCB Design

Schematic capture, board layout, gerber file generation, and circuit simulation using softwares such as KiCAD, DipTrace, Cadence, and LTspice.

Embedded Software

Low level programming of ARM Cortex-M microcontrollers in C and assembly including peripheral management of ADCs, UARTs, I2C/SPI networks, and GPIO.

Programming

High level programming of cross-platform user interfaces and PC tools using APIs and C++/Python libraries

Leadership

Project management, documentation, and coordination in multidisciplinary engineering teams including usage of management software such as Jira, Git/GitHub, Bitbucket, and ClickUp.
