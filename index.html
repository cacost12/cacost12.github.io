About Me

Software/Electrical Engineer

Hi, my name is Colton and I am a graduate from Arizona State University with a degree in electrical engineering. I am an amateur rocketry enthusiast, and am level 2 certified through Tripoli. I am primarily interested in avionics, and have industry experience in aviation, defense, and consumer electronics. In college, I was very involved in the community at ASU working on collaborative, student-led projects and research. I mainly worked with Sun Devil Rocketry, where I held several leadership positions such as President and Liquid Propulsion lead. I also founded the Avionics Team, which I started in order to apply my interests in electronics and software to ongoing projects within the organization.

Since college, I have continued to work on rocketry projects in my free time. My technical work has primarily revolved around the development of flight computers for high power/amateur rockets which control recovery systems, collect flight data, add redundancy options, and more. Look at the projects posted below for more information.
